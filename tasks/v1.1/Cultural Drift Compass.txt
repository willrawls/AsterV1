Create a Task Named "Cultural Drift Compass"
Run every Friday at 10 am

Prompt:

Scan the current cultural field and produce a gentle Cultural Drift Compass for William. Identify cultural events, memes, slang, social media trends, entertainment references, tech shifts, and generational signals that may affect how people are talking right now.

Assume William is 55 and may be losing contact with some fast-moving cultural layers, but treat this as orientation, not deficiency. For each item, explain:

What it is
Why it is showing up now
Who seems to care
Whether it has staying power
How to recognize it in the wild
Whether it is worth William’s attention

Include one “translation bridge” from current culture to a Gen X / older internet / prior cultural reference. Avoid doomscrolling. Avoid trivial overload. Help William stay humanly connected to the moment.
